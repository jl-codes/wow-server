How to Join

Get started in just 4 simple steps. You'll be exploring Azeroth in no time!

1

Download the Game Client

You need the WoW 3.3.5a client (about 15 GB). This is the Wrath of the Lich King version — NOT the modern retail WoW.

📥 Download from ChromieCraft (Recommended)
📥 Download from Warmane (Alternative)

⚠️ If you see a 100GB+ download, that's the wrong version! The 3.3.5a client is only ~15 GB.

2

Set the Realmlist

Navigate to your WoW folder → Data → enUS (or enGB) → open realmlist.wtf with Notepad.

Delete everything and replace with:

set realmlist 127.0.0.1

If connecting from another computer on the network, use the server's local IP address instead.

3

Get Your Account

Ask the server admin to create an account for you, or if you are the admin, open the worldserver console and type:

account create <username> <password>
4

Launch & Play!

Run Wow.exe from your game folder. Do NOT use the Launcher! Log in with your account credentials, create a character, and begin your adventure!
